GlobFace Child Safety Standards

Zero Tolerance Policy for Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

Our Commitment

GlobFace is committed to providing a safe and respectful environment for all users. We strictly prohibit any form of child sexual abuse material (CSAM), exploitation, or harmful behavior involving minors.

Zero Tolerance Policy

  • No sexual content involving minors is allowed.
  • No grooming, exploitation, or solicitation of minors.
  • No sharing, uploading, or distributing CSAM.
  • Immediate action is taken against violating accounts.

Protection Measures

  • User reporting and blocking system available in-app.
  • Account suspension and permanent bans for violations.
  • Monitoring and moderation of user-generated content.
  • Cooperation with law enforcement agencies when required.

Reporting Child Safety Concerns

Users can report any child safety concerns directly within the GlobFace app. All reports are reviewed promptly and appropriate action is taken.

Legal Compliance

GlobFace complies with all applicable local, national, and international child safety laws and reports confirmed violations to relevant authorities.
