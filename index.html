Privacy Policy for Globeface

App Name: Globeface

Company Name: Advik Digital Studio

Country: India (Available worldwide)

1. Information We Collect

When you use Globeface, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Name, Email, Profile Photo (during signup/login)

  • User Content: Photos, Videos, Reels, Titles, Descriptions you upload

  • Chat ID: A unique Chat ID is generated at login to allow chatting with other users

  • Optional Additional Data: Date of Birth and other optional info you provide

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide app services such as posting, sharing, chatting, and searching content
  • Authenticate and manage user accounts

3. Data Sharing
