Delete Account – GlobeFace

Developer: Advik Digital Studio

How to request account deletion

  1. Send an email from your registered email address
  2. Email subject: Delete My GlobeFace Account
  3. Send to: advikdigitalstudio@gmail.com

Data that will be deleted

  • User profile (name, photo)
  • Posts, reels, stories
  • Chats and shared media

Data retention

Some basic logs may be retained for up to 30 days for security and legal purposes, after which they are permanently deleted.

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 days.
